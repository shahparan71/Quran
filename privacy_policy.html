Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Welcome to Muslim Guide. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Muslim Guide and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Muslim Guide is designed to provide you with Islamic tools and information. To function correctly, we may access the following information:

  • Location Data: We request access to your device's precise location (GPS and network-based) to provide accurate Prayer Times and Qibla direction based on your current coordinates.
  • Device Storage: We request permission to access your device's storage to download and store Quran audio files and PDF documents for offline use.
  • Settings & Preferences: We store your app settings (such as theme choice, font size, and translation preferences) locally on your device to ensure a consistent experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To calculate prayer times accurately for your location.
  • To provide the Qibla compass functionality.
  • To allow you to download and listen to Quran recitations offline.
  • To maintain your preferred app configuration.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties. All location calculations are performed on the device or via secure API calls that do not store your identification.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app updates and basic functionality on Android devices.

4. Data Storage

Most of your data (settings, last read position, etc.) is stored locally on your device using industry-standard storage methods (SharedPreferences and SQLite). If you uninstall the app, this data is typically removed.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our records.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
